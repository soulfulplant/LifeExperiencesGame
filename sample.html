How the samples work

Each sample is supplied in two free PDF layouts. Choose the compact version to print multiple card sides on each sheet, or the full-page version to print one card front or back per page.

Download your preferred layout, print the cards, cut them out where needed and play using the included instructions.

The Print & Play Demo is designed for quick casual play.
The Create Your Own Pack lets you write custom cards.
The Conversation Starter Pack is designed for classrooms, tutors and group discussion.
