How To Play

1

Draw

Draw an Experience Card and read it aloud. Brace yourself.

2

React

Play a Reaction Card and read it out. Or play your Story Card by telling a real story that fits the Experience.

3

Vote

Hand your Reaction Card to whoever made you laugh or cringe the hardest. Any cards awarded to you become your points.

4

Score

Story Cards and Full Houses can earn bonus points.

5

Rotate

Top your hand back up to 6 Reaction Cards. Pass the draw to the player on your left.
