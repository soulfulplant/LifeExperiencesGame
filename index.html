How To Play

1

Draw

Draw an Experience Card and read it aloud. Brace yourself.

2

React

Play a Reaction Card and read it out. Or play your Story Card by telling a real story that fits the Experience.

3

Vote

Everyone decides at the same time, pointing at their chosen player, then hands over their Reaction Card simultaneously. No bandwagon voting. Just judgement.

4

Score

Story Cards and Full Houses can earn bonus points.

5

Refresh & Rotate

Top your hand back up to 6 Reaction Cards, then you may discard and replace any number of cards before the next round. Then pass the Experience draw to the player on your left.
